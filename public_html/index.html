An Introduction to Asia Research & Engagement

ARE promotes investor-company dialogue on environmental, social, and governance (ESG) issues. ARE works with investors, companies, banks, not-for-profits, and regulators to improve corporate governance and to transition to more sustainable economies.

ARE works with its clients to understand their needs, identify key issues, develop research or engagement strategies, build coalitions of stakeholders, and create the momentum for appropriate change.

ARE provides high quality bespoke services for investors, banks, and NGOs considering the business and investment context of companies operating in Asia. ARE also helps companies communicate their message on ESG to investors.
