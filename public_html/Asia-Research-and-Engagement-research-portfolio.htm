Thematic reports:

Sustainable Stock Exchanges II

Commissioned by Aviva and working with UNCTAD. The report was launched at Rio +20. NYSE Euronext recently joined the Sustainable Stock Exchange Initiative, including a commitment to bringing in ESG disclosure by issuers on the exchanges.

The Future of Fish in Asia

The David and Lucile Packard Foundation commissioned this report, which won the 2011 Farsight award for the best ESG/ Financial report of the year. It provides investors with a template for engaging companies across the Asian seafood supply chains to improve the sustainability of their production and sourcing.

Feeding Asia

Arisaig, a boutique investor that focuses exclusively on listed consumer businesses in emerging markets, commissioned this report.

Good Companies Guide 2010 – How sustainability affects business

This report, published by the Observer, looked at the FTSE 350 from ecological and societal perspectives. It considered for each company whether related issues were likely to present opportunities or risks and how strongly.

Good Companies Guide 2009 – Gender balance in Britain plc.

This report, published by the Observer, ranked FTSE 350 companies on gender balance. It considered the board composition as well as the depth and breadth of equal opportunities related disclosure.
