About Asia Research & Engagement

ARE provides research and representation services to investors, banks and NGOs on Asian listed companies looking at environmental, social and governance (ESG). ARE provides high quality bespoke services considering the business and investment context of companies in the Asian listed space.

For international investors with holdings in the region, ARE provides understanding of Asian perspectives and represents investor concerns on ESG issues in meetings with companies and regulators.

For Asian investors, ARE provides advice how to build ESG research into their investment processes, whether that to make money or to accomplish broader goals. This includes evaluating research and benchmarking services providers.

For banks, ARE provides advisory services on implementing ESG considerations into credit policy and assessment processes to safeguard reputation and understand emerging risk areas.

For NGOs, ARE provides assistance in engaging with the financial sector, assessing research needs and designing engagement that will help NGOs achieve their goals.

For companies, to maintain its independence ARE does not provide services directly to companies outside of financial services, but is happy to discuss ESG issues in exchange for broadening its network. ARE’s deep insights can also be accessed through partner organisations.

In addition, ARE runs research and engagement projects to support the adoption of sustainable practices by Asian companies.
